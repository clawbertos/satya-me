Every COO interview hits the question: *"Have you ever transformed support into a revenue lever?"* August 2016 is my answer.

## Situation
- Support backlog: 1,840 tickets >24h.
- NPS stuck at 18, executives avoided customer calls.
- CCO role vacant; CEO needed someone to own the turnaround before the next renewal season.

## Intervention
1. **Ops ownership:** Moved support under the COO office for 120 days and set joint targets with Sales (expansion) and Product (defect burn-down).
2. **Playbooked tiers:** Introduced triage pods (Rescue, Stabilize, Expand) with SLAs and auto-suggested actions.
3. **Success analytics:** Instrumented health scoring that flowed straight into renewal forecasts.
4. **Talent shift:** Retained top 30% agents, reskilled them into success architects; backfilled Tier 1 with an outsourced partner.

## Outcome
- Backlog down 72% in 45 days.
- NPS climbed from 18 to 42 by quarter end.
- Expansion pipeline attributed $3.1M ARR to the new success motion.
